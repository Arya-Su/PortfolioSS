About me

Hello. My name is Samra. I am 29 years old, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Physics. After completing my studies, I traditionally, like most of my colleagues from my studies, started my career in education. Even though I found the job of a teacher interesting, in a short time I summed up all the aspects of that profession that attracted me (foreign plans and programs, headteacher activities, excursions, solving youth problems and a class full of critical thinking, science and laughter). From the first meeting with a computer (an old Pentium 1 from my father's work), I was the main "hacker" in the family (I knew how to defragment a disk).

I spent the last 10 years of my life in the eternal dilemma between IT and teaching (and the two could always go together, right?). In January of this year, I realized that IT is definitely the sector in which I want to continue building my career. I quit my "secure" job that was 5 minutes away from my apartment and started learning programming languages. I already had foundations in HTML, CSS and JavaScript (I completed the courses), so I decided to explore Python. Python and I clicked at the very start, and months later it is my favorite, even though the labor market has always led me away from it. I researched a couple of frameworks, including those related to data analysis, but I never went beyond the basics.
